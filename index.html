Introduction to Fira Sans

The typeface aims to be legible, covering a wide range of handsets “varying in screen quality and rendering.”

The designer of this typeface is a company called Carrois Apostrophe, which was founded in 1975 by Ralph Carrois. The company’s original interest was in Graphic Design but changed to Type Design while creating corporate typefaces for companies.

Fira Sans was a reimagining of the typeface FF Meta. The humanist sans-serif typeface of Fira Sans takes the utility of Meta and makes it better rendered for multiple types of screens.

Designed by
Ralph Carrois
